Rhinoceros

Adult rhinos have no natural predators, except for man.

  • Fact 1: Ceratotherium simum
  • Fact 2: 11-13 feet
  • Fact 3: 40 years
  • Fact 4: Grassland and open savanna

The African rhino is divided into two species, the black rhino and the white rhino. White rhinos mainly live in South Africa, but they have also been reintroduced to Botswana, Namibia, Swaziland, and Zimbabwe.
